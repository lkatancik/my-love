💌 Letters to You

There are no words that can truly capture what I feel for you. Every time I try to explain it, the words feel too small, too simple to hold the weight of what you mean to me. It is not just love, it is something deeper, something that has become a part of who I am. You are not just someone I love, you are the place where I feel most myself, the person who sees every side of me and still stays, still chooses me every day. Our love is not just about the good moments, though those are endless and beautiful. It is about how we move through everything together, how even in the quiet or chaotic moments there is this unshakable sense of us. We don’t need to try to be something we are not, because what we are together is already everything. I look at you and I feel like I’m looking at the one thing in this world that is truly real, something that can never be replaced or replicated. When I think about us, I don’t just see love as people write about it in books or songs. I see something living, something we’ve built without even realizing it, something stronger than the words I could use to describe it. You are my heart, my safe place, and the one person who feels like home. Nothing else compares to that feeling, and I don’t think it ever could.
